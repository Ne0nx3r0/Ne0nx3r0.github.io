YOU ARE NOT SAFE

( And that's okay )

"How secure should it be?"
Most people would say "totally secure!"
( that outta do it! )
But that's not possible because security has costs:
Time
Money
Convenience
Capability
Freedom
Which means...
Perfect security will cost you ALL
your time, money, conveniences,
capabilities and freedoms.
So what do we do?
We make trade-offs, everyday:
Drive to work,
risk car accident
Visit a public place,
risk getting the flu
Work around the house
risk injury / death
Go to the ocean,
risk shark attack
Fly in a plane,
risk it crashing
The last two tend to be the scariest

Which is strange because...
